3. Vehicle Data Privacy & Telemetry

We prioritize your data sovereignty. MyGiulia and the Telemetry Analyzer are built to ensure your car's data remains yours.

  • Local Browser Processing: When using the My Giulia Telemetry Analyzer, all data files (log files, CSVs, or telemetry) are analyzed locally within your web browser. Your vehicle data is never uploaded to our servers.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transmit your vehicle's telemetry, diagnostic data, or GPS coordinates to the developer, third parties, or any external cloud services.
  • Offline Capability: The core analysis logic is designed to function without the need to send your sensitive vehicle information over the internet.
